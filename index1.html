Einstein once said,
"The only source of knowledge is experience."

At Hacknovate 1.0, we bring together India’s best coders, developers, designers, innovators, creators and entrepreneurs of tomorrow. Hacknovate is geared up for those who are passionate about building, designing and innovating. It is a 12 Hr event where the enthusiasts meet, develop and compete in the product prototyping competition.

Next

FinTech

Your goal is to come up with a technically feasible and economic solution, that can contribute in digitizing the Indian economy. Your goal is to design and develop system that can make digital payment a new normal for every Indian citizen. Come up with ideas that can make digital payments more secure and easy for people from every corner of the country. Your solution should be workable, secure and scalable.

Next

Smart communication/citizen service

With mass urbanization, people face an ever-increasing demand for better service and quality of life. Create solution that can provide a platform that promote collaborative use of citizen services among the people. The system should empower people to communicate, discuss, mobilize and ultimately solve their communtiy issues in collaboration with the government.

Next

Waste management

A growing population and economy means increased volumes of waste generated. This puts pressure on waste management facilities, which are already in short supply. Create solution that leverage existing waste management services smartly and leads to efficient collection, management and disposal of waste especially in urban areas. Such system(hardware/software) will contribute to the smart city development scheme of the Indian government.

Next

Energy Efficiency

Energy need is ever-growing. As population is increasing, the demand for sustainable and renewable energy is increasing. Innovate new and efficient system to generate, store and consume the energy in urban areas.

Next

Smart Healthcare system

In India, the primary focus is on affordability and accessibility of healthcare to all. Under this theme, students need to innovate new products and make them in India to enhance affordability and access of healthcare solutions.

Next

Open Innovation

"Innovation seems no boundary."
New and innovative iddeas that can change the face of urbanization in India are invited in this category. The solution must be sustainable, efficient, economical and working.
